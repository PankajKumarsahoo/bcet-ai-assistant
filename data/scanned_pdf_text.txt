## 6. ADMISSION PROCESS (OJEE / JEE MAIN)

### Who Can Apply
- **JEE Main rank holders** → B.Tech, B.Plan, B.Arch, B.Pharm, B.CAT, Int. MSc
- **OJEE-2024 qualified** → All other programs

### Step-by-Step Counselling Process
1. **Registration** – Visit www.ojee.nic.in, click "New Candidate Registration"
2. **Counselling Fee** – Pay ₹450/- online only
3. **Choice Filling** – Add colleges and branches in order of preference
4. **Mock Allotment** – Check indicative allotment status
5. **Choice Locking** – Lock choices using your own password *(irreversible — cannot be changed after locking)*
6. **Round-I Allotment** – View allotment result online / SMS
7. **Post-Allotment Steps:**
   - Choose Freeze/Float option for seat upgradation
   - Upload required documents (PDF format only)
   - Pay Part Admission / Seat Confirmation Fee
8. **Document Verification** – Done online; queries resolved within deadline
9. **Round-II Allotment** – New allotments or upgrades
10. **Round-III Allotment** – Final open round (for 2nd/Special OJEE students too)
11. **Final Allotment Letter** – Download and report to allotted college

### Part Admission Fee
| Category | Fee |
|----------|-----|
| General | ₹10,000 |
| SC / ST / PC | ₹5,000 |

### Withdrawal & Refund
- Withdrawal refunds ₹5,000 or ₹10,000 (as deposited) if done within schedule
- **No refund** after the withdrawal deadline under any circumstances
- Candidates who paid and did NOT withdraw but also did NOT report — no refund

### TFW (Tuition Fee Waiver) Scheme
- For candidates whose parents' annual income < ₹8 Lakhs
- Applicable to: B.Tech, B.Pharm, B.Plan, LE B.Tech, LE Pharm, MBA, MCA
- Only for native Odisha candidates
- Must submit income certificate (Appendix-VII / online issued)
- Limited to 5% supernumerary of sanctioned intake
